About Us

all began when we crossed paths at school in Taiwan in 2014. Minh was there for his exchange semester, and we happened to be in the same class. Little did we know that this chance encounter would set the stage for a beautiful love story. After Minh completed his studies six months later, our long-distance relationship began. Despite the miles that separated us, our love only grew stronger, and after nearly a decade of shared dreams, laughter, and adventures, we tied the knot in Taiwan in 2023. Now, we cannot wait to celebrate our love story with our cherished family and friends in the beautiful setting of Germany.
